Our Haircut Types, Services and Styles

Haircut style for Men

Bald Fade

It sleek look that adds a smart appearance and can be paired with a wide range of cuts. Options are a low bald fade, understated look or a high bald fade for contrast.

Bowl Cut

the style features a straight cut fringe with the sides and back of the hair cut to the same length. Today, the modern bowl cut is less severe undercut.

Burst Fade

The look can be defined by the hair tapering around the ear and the back of the head, creating a rounded, semi-circular appearance and longer at the neck.

Faux Hawk

It is emulate a great modern interpretation of the classic punk trend. The look features short sides and a longer strip of hair on top that is styled upwards.

Haircut style for Woman

Long Shag

It adds layers and movement showing dimension while taking some of the weight out, making a long shag haircut ideal for those with thicker hair.

Short Shag

It is timelessly chic! Can be also adapted to suit most face shapes. Depending on the hair length, the placement of the layers, and also whether you add a fringe.

Straight Hair

The original shag haircut was on straight hair, so the layers are the most visible on ladies with this hair type. For that reason, it also a looks great on fine hair.

Blunt Bangs

The shag hairstyle is created with choppy layers and fullness at the crown. It can be also cut on various hair textures and adapted to different lengths.

Haircut style for Children and Seniors

Comb Over

The comb over for kids is one of the most versatile and best haircuts to get. Specifically, the comb over fade provides the foundation to a number of hairstyles.

Elegant Chignon

Updos with tucked in ends, such as low buns, chignons and French knots look elegant and can be worn as a casual or formal hairstyle. It is classic and conservative.
