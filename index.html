Policies & Procedures

Children

We welcome children to the salon, provided a parent or guardian be actively watching them at all times. This policy is in place to ensure your child's safety from hazards such as hot tools, sharp tools and dangerous chemicals present in the salon at all times.

Parking

Street parking available on John Doe ave and adjacent streets. Parking is also sometimes available on Sir John Franklin Rd alongside the railway tracks. Please do not park in any nearby lots (John Doe animal hospital, or Jn's/RBC) as you risk being towed.

Cancellations/No Shows

Out of respect for your stylist we ask that you give a minimum of 24 hr notice for all cancellations. Cancellations without 24hr notice/no shows will incur a fee calculated at 50% of the service cost originally booked.

Payments and Exchanges

We accept Visa, MasterCard, debit, and cash. If you are unhappy with a product you purchased, we will happily exchange it for you provided it is at least 70% full with its original contents.

Refunds

We do not offer refunds on services. If you are unhappy with your service please consult with your stylist. 'Redos' and/or fixes will only be performed by the stylist by which the original appointment was made with.

Deposits

Upon booking certain high value services a stylist may ask you for a deposit. This may include, but is not limited to: colours, highlights, extensions, and bridal party bookings.
