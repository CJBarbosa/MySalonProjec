Meet the Stylist

Educated and award winning stylist with incredible talent.

With awards in the hair industry and extensive advanced education in colours, cuts, texture and design, a creative hair stylist have the ability to enhance and nurture ones own personal style. These skills are transferred to our clients giving them on trend, sophisticated and contemporary looks highlighted by exceptional service

Angelina Saintagostino

I’m proud to be the owner of My Salon, I’ve had a passion for this industry for over 10 yrs. Over the course of my career, I have been fortunate enough to meet, be inspired, and influenced by countless industry leaders.

My philosophy is the same with a “hair artist.” It’s not only what we create but what we reveal as a style for our clients.

My favourite aspect of working in the hair industry is having so many opportunities to learn from the talented people around me. I think one of the most important parts of being a successful stylist is to always be open to learning something new so that I can continue offering something fresh and current to my clients. Some of my most memorable experiences have been working on fashion/hair shows and photo shoots with my peers.
